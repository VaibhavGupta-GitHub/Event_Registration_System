Help Section

Need assistance? Here you can find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use the Event Registration System:

  • Viewing Events: Navigate to 'View Events' to see upcoming and current events.
  • Creating Events: Go to 'Create Event' to add a new event to the system.
  • Booking Tickets: Use 'Book Ticket' to reserve your spot at an event.
  • Viewing Tickets: Check your booked tickets under 'View Tickets'.
